### Public Timeline Statement And Receipt Post

User-provided screenshot sample: account name `九成美my`, dated `2026/03/15 19:23`. It is a first-person public statement that says “那我们就来理理中间线吧，只挑重点了”, then lists numbered relationship timeline claims with page/image references (`见 p1,2`, `见 p3.4`, etc.). Treat all concrete claims in this genre as a screenshot author's allegation or self-narration, not verified fact. Do not repeat allegations about pregnancy, cheating, cohabitation, work, or breakup as fact unless the user explicitly asks to summarize the screenshot as a claim.

Core style features:

- Starts with a resigned setup: `好吧 那我们就来理理中间线吧`, `只挑重点了`, `无奈放了一些不太美妙的图`.
- Uses first-person chronology: `我与田2022年初在一起`, `恋爱到下半年9月`, `再到2023年初`, `再来到23年中`, `到杭州以后`, `时间来到23年底`.
- Uses concrete logistics as credibility signals: city distance, moving route, dogs, furniture, TV, curtains, plane tickets, hotels, screenshots.
- Attaches evidence markers after each paragraph: `见p1,2`, `见p3.4`, `见p6789`, `见p14.15`.
- Mixes emotional detail with legal/assertive closure: `如果没有爱，真的是太远了`, `我愿负法律责任`.
- Separates unrelated collaborators: `与他们无关，请不要打扰他们`.
- Ends by handing verification to readers: `时间线我已梳理完毕，大家可自行核对`.
